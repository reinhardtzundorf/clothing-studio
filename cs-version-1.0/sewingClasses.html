Group Sewing Classes

Group sewing lessons are offered in the structure of a relaxed social group where ideas are shared and guidance is offered as needed in order to keep you on the way of sewing the clothing item that you want.

The Clothing Studio's Group Sewing Lesons are once a week and give students an opportunity to bring the ideas that they want to make into clothing garments to a supportive and self-paced environment.

Enthusiasts, first time sewing students and the experienced are all welcome.

What do I bring?

Your project. An idea, more ideas, material and a pattern.

If you don't have all of these, we can help you as long as you know what you want to make.

Fees & Times

Group Sewing Lessons are R 250 per month.

Sewing enthusiasts are expected to pay monthly at the beginning of each month on the first Wednesday's sewing class.

Wednesday Mornings 09h00 - 12h00

Wednesday Evenings 18h30 - 21h00
