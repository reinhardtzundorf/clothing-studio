Piano String Covers

The inner workings of pianos are susceptible to the elements. An oft needed and neglected accessory is a piano string cover. String covers, if specifically crafted and adjusted according to the dimensions of the instrument, improve on the longivity of tuning and helps reduce the formation of rust on the piano's strings.

The Clothing Studio makes handmade piano string covers for pianos of any size and shape.

About Ordering

The Piano String Covers are custom made and require a waiting period of 10 days.
